About Us

Who we are?

This is where creativity is unleashed- State-of-the-art BusinessBoxx is a one stop solution for all your business essentials. BusinessBoxx is a premier collaborative work space- shared & private offices, meeting rooms and associated amenities/facilites. We are creators, leaders and self-starters. We try new things, we challenge convention and we are not afraid to fail.

What we do?

For Start-ups and emerging enterprises we create environment that improves productivity, innovation and collaboration. The world class business center is launched for the very first time with the clear objective of providing fully serviced premium work spaces for every business need. BusinessBoxx is strategically located on Anand-VidhyaNagar Road keeping our users smallest need in mind.

Why choose us?

We take away many of the facilities management, real estate and capital expenditure headaches that have affected businesses in the past, replacing them with flexibility, agility and the ability to focus on business essentials. Access to latest technology with 24/7 technical support and a business class infrastructure. Flexible lease with minimal lead time no set-up cost.

Our Products

VIRTUAL OFFICE


Get a professional address and establish the presence in the market with a virtual office. Our plans include mail handling service, access to conference room and more. All inclusive pricing. Just show up when needed.

OFFICE SPACE

Use our conference room for interviews, presentations, client pitches or employee training. We provide conference room by the hour. Catering, coffee service, projection equipment and other services are available to ensure you have everything for your meeting. Just show up and get started.
